Project Name: WorldSurf

The WorldSurf App

The WorldSurf App is designed to allow ocean enthusiasts all over the world check surf and tide conditions of local beaches. After download, a quick sign-up process enables users to check current conditions in any area by city name or zip code. Current weather is included, along with surf forecasts up to 14 days in the future. This award winning app has been downloaded over 500,000 times on apple devices and is now being introduced to Android markets.
